About us

With a stay at our Hotel, BW Signature Collection in Anaconda, you'll be a 5-minute walk from Anaconda Smoke Stack State Park and within a 5-minute drive of Copper Village Museum & Arts Center. This hotel is 1.3 mi (2.2 km) from Old Works Golf Course and 1.6 mi (2.6 km) from Washoe Theatre.

Take advantage of recreational opportunities offered, including an indoor pool, a spa tub, and a 24-hour fitness center. Additional features at this hotel include complimentary wireless Internet access and a picnic area. Featured amenities include a 24-hour business center, express check-out, and a 24-hour front desk. Free self parking is available onsite.
